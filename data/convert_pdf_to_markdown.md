## Marker

Marker converts documents to markdown, JSON, chunks, and HTML quickly and accurately.

### marker-pdf

Best quality conversion, specifically designed for LLM use cases.

### Convert multiple files

```sh
marker data/pdf
```

**Example output:**
```
marker: Converting 1 pdfs in chunk 1/1 with 1 processes and saving to ixora-chatbot\venv\Lib\site-packages\conversion_results
```
